# **Kung Pao Chicken Recipe**

## **Ingredients**

### *Main Ingredients*:
- 300g chicken breast (diced)
- 50g peanuts (unsalted)
- 2 green onions (chopped)
- 1 bell pepper (diced)

### *Sauce Ingredients*:
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch (mixed with water to form a slurry)
